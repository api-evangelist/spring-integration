On behalf of the team and everyone who contributed, I am pleased to announce the availability of Spring Data 2025.0.13 . This service release is expected to be the final open-source release of the Spring Data 3.5.x generation and contains regression fixes only. Please consider upgrading to the latest 4.0.x ( 2025.1.x release train) or 4.1.x release at your earliest convenience.
